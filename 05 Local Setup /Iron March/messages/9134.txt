
<blockquote data-ipsquote="" class="ipsQuote" data-ipsquote-username="Victory of the People" data-cite="Victory of the People" data-ipsquote-timestamp="1431562404"><div><div><p>Makes sense to ban alcohol, but why coffee?</p></div></div></blockquote>
<p>It was a drink that was usually drunk in coffee houses, which were places of idleness, where men would go to drink coffee smoke shisha and engage in activities like gambling and listening to dervishes, bards or meddahs(wandering storytellers).</p>
<p>Banning the drink would mean to close down these places aswell.</p>
