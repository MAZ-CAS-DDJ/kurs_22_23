<p>This book is a concise overview of Spengler's life and influences. The author also addresses major criticisms and evaluates Spengler's predictions. In addition, the author discusses Spengler's unpublished THIRD volume of Decline of the West. Man and Technics was intended to be an introduction to his third volume that was never published because of his untimely death.</p>
